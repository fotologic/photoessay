Boxes to open
Out there · PhotoPedagogy open ↗
Threshold Concepts for Photography
The full set. Big ideas about what photography is and does, meant to unsettle the obvious and give an investigation something to think with. Not a checklist. A place to find the concept your work has been circling without your knowing its name.
Out there · Google Drive open ↗
Previous students' essays
Real essays by students who sat where you are sitting. They will not show you how to write yours, because yours is about something only you are investigating. What they show is what genuine commitment to a subject looks like on the page.
Out there · a full site open ↗
An example website
A complete A-level photography site, put together the way yours might be. Useful less as a template than as proof of what a finished, thought-through body of work looks like when someone has committed to it.
Out there · ArtPedagogy open ↗
Writing about images
Sound, practical advice on the personal study and how to write about photographs with attention rather than cliché. Worth reading early, and again halfway through when you have forgotten it.
